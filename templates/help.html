Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Astrology is the study of celestial bodies and their symbolic influence on human life. It offers insights into personality and life events by interpreting planetary positions.
Your zodiac sign is based on your birth date. Each of the 12 zodiac signs corresponds to a specific date range, such as Aries (March 21 – April 19).
Astrology offers patterns and tendencies based on celestial events, not fixed predictions. It’s a tool for insight, not fortune-telling.
Western astrology uses the tropical zodiac, while Vedic (Jyotish) uses the sidereal zodiac. Both systems have different interpretations and methods.
Provide your birth date, time, and place to an astrologer or online tool. They’ll generate a birth chart and offer personalized readings.
